How it works

1. Intake: you describe the issue (brief form or email). 2. Clarify: I identify key questions and evidence needed. 3. Research & draft: I prepare a concise brief and draft documents. 4. Review: you check and request edits. 5. Final: I deliver a final document and a short checklist.

Pricing

This service operates on a pay‑what‑you‑want basis. You decide what you can afford after receiving the work. This approach keeps support accessible while recognising the value of time, care and expertise.

Ethics & Scope

I provide information, analysis and drafting support — not legal advice. I protect client data, work with consent, and use secure tools. If legal representation is needed, I can help a client prepare materials for a qualified adviser.
